JufoPay is a digital financial services platform focused on making payments, transfers, utility bills, and wallet services easy and accessible for users and small businesses.

## Overview

JufoPay enables users to perform essential financial activities in real time, including:

- Sending and receiving money instantly
- Paying for utilities like electricity, cable, and internet
- Purchasing airtime and data
- Using mobile wallet services
- Acting as a local agent and accessing business financial tools

## Key Features

- **Money Transfers:** Fast, secure transfers between users.
- **Utility Payments:** Pay bills directly through the platform.
- **Airtime & Data:** Purchase mobile credits with ease.
- **Agent & Business Services:** Support for agents and business accounts.
- **Offline/USSD Support:** Access core features without a smartphone.

## Built With

JufoPay’s platform delivers interactive financial services across web and mobile, designed for performance and accessibility.
